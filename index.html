Eye Acuity Test

Test Instructions

  1. Make sure you will be undisturbed for the duration of the test
  2. Stay arms length from your screen
  3. Sit up straight with your back against your chair
  4. Do not squint or strain your eyes
  5. Read the letters out loud
  6. If you are unsure about the letter, just say "Don't know"
  7. Start with your LEFT EYE covered and click "I Understand" to start the test
