Test Instructions

  1. Make sure you will be undisturbed for the duration of the test
  2. Stay arms length from your screen
  3. Sit up straight with your back against your chair
  4. Turn your screen brightness all the way up
  5. Do not squint or strain your eyes
  6. Read the letters out loud
  7. If you are unsure about the letter, just say "Don't know"
  8. Start with your LEFT EYE covered and click "I Understand" when you are ready. The test will begin immediately
